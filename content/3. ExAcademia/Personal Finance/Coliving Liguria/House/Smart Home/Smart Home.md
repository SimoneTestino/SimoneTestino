## Audio
The audio system features multiple [Speaker](https://www.home-assistant.io/integrations/symfonisk/) units strategically placed for whole-home sound, with potential integration for microphones to enable hands-free voice control, phone calls, or AI chatbot interactions throughout the house. **4 × €150 = €600**

## Cleaning
Automated cleaning will be handled by a [Roomba Integration](https://www.home-assistant.io/integrations/roomba/), allowing the robotic vacuum to be controlled and scheduled through the home automation system for seamless integration with other smart home routines. **€150**

## Lighting
The lighting system includes smart bulbs for main rooms, LED strips for accent lighting, and automated roller shades for natural light control, creating a comprehensive lighting solution that can be programmed for different moods and times of day. **Smart Bulbs: 2 × €0 + 4 × €15 = €60, Smart Stripes: 2 × €30 = €60, Roller Shade: 4 × €30 = €120**

## Air Control
Environmental monitoring and control will be managed through various sensors including humidity sensors, thermometers, fans, barometers, CO₂ sensors, and pellet heating control integration, ensuring optimal air quality and temperature throughout the home. **Total: €70**

## Doors
Access control features an external smart lock for keyless entry and internal door sensors with opening systems for automated door control and security monitoring. **External Lock: €200, Inside Door Sensor & Opening System: €50**

## Security
The security system comprises external cameras for perimeter monitoring, external lights for deterrent and safety purposes, potentially including security flash capabilities for enhanced protection. **External Cameras: €25 × 4 = €100, External Lights: €30 × 2 = €60**

## Sensors
Additional sensing capabilities include proximity sensors for presence detection, thermometers for fire alarm integration, and barometers for weather monitoring and atmospheric pressure tracking. **Proximity Sensors: €15 × 3 = €45, Thermometers & Barometers: €15 × 3 = €45**

## Interactions
User interaction will be facilitated through an iPad installation for centralised control and physical buttons positioned throughout the home for convenient manual control of various systems and scenes. **iPad Installation: €30, Buttons: €30**
