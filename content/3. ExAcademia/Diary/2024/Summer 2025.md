#### Fixing the Dates for Sydney & Beijing

The free period in Sydney is from 30.06.2025 at 10:00 to 24.07.2025 at 07:00. This opens up two possible two-week time slots for Beijing:
- **Before Sydney**: From 16.06.2025 to 30.06.2025.
- **After Sydney**: From 24.07.2025 to 07.08.2025.

There are significant disadvantages to the first option. Firstly, there is a potential clash with the resit exams (if the hotel is booked by the university, requesting changes might be complicated). Secondly, I need to ensure that the project I will present (either my [[Master Thesis]] or [[Forcing on Urelements (Research Project)]]) is at an advanced stage before leaving. Lastly, opting for the second set of dates would also ensure that I have already purchased the [[Buying MacBook Air 15 inch]], which will make working without a monitor considerably easier. Consequently, I conclude that it is best to depart for Sydney around the very beginning of July and travel to Beijing from 24.07.2025 until 07.08.2025.
