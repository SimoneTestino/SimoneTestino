> Luna is an innovative helium balloon lighting system designed to revolutionize exterior illumination for construction sites, events, and emergency operations. Combining superior light quality with breakthrough affordability, Luna offers a compelling alternative to traditional light towers through advanced engineering and sustainable design.

## Vision and Innovation

The Luna project reimagines portable lighting by addressing fundamental inefficiencies in traditional light towers. Our solution delivers **exceptional illumination quality** from heights up to 50 meters while maintaining **dramatically lower costs** and **minimal environmental impact**. Every design decision is validated through rigorous mathematical modeling and simulation before physical prototyping.

## Legal and Regulatory Status

Luna is classified as a **tethered gas balloon for commercial operations** under European aviation regulations. Our comprehensive regulatory research has engaged:

- **EASA** (European Union Aviation Safety Agency): CS 31GB.28 classification
- **ENAC** (Italian Civil Aviation Authority): Formal inquiry submitted May 2025

We are currently awaiting official guidance on operational boundaries for construction sites and event venues. See [[Legal/Regulatory Research|complete regulatory analysis]] and [[Legal/Patent Information|patent details]].
