### **2. What does community mean to you?**

Community means two things: **Teamwork** and **Social Life**.

* **Teamwork and Co-Creation (The Builder's Spirit):**
    * It's about **diverse skills** and **contributions**. We are building a "blank canvas" from the ground up, requiring **deep cleaning, painting, and gardening**.
    * We value the **"doer, the builder, and the person who isn't afraid to get their hands dirty"**.
    * **Inclusivity and Variety:** We have members from different continents, with ages ranging from under 17 to 70. The fun is in discovering how **everyone has an opportunity to contribute** and what unique skills they bring. **This variety is our strength.**

* **Social Life and Revitalisation (The Heart of the Project):**
    * Organising **events, shared meals, and activities** to create a vibrant life in a place that would otherwise be quiet.
    * Making the property the **"heart of our community"** where residents and activities converge.

### **3. How do travellers shape your project and how does your project shape them?**

Travellers/Residents are the **core** of the project, both for the **initial building** and the **long-term financial model**.

* **Shaping the Project (The Doers):**
    * Travellers are the **"Community Residents (Volunteer Spirit)"** who contribute to daily community life (cooking, cleaning, light maintenance) in exchange for **very low rent**. They are our hands-on builders who help bring the rustic houses **"to life"**.
    * *Legal Note to Self: Remind them that volunteers are legally **"Residents"** under a rental contract, as per the legal framework*.

* **Shaping Them (The Customers):**
    * The project provides a productive, **budget-friendly place** for **Digital Nomads / Remote Workers** with high-speed internet.
    * It's a hub for **learning and adventure**, hosting activities and projects like **Italian Teacher** and **Mountain Bike Guide**. They are shaped by the opportunities to live, connect, and **build**.

### **4. What does sustainability look like in your world?**

Sustainability is a **long-term vision** and a **commitment** to respect our environment, which is directly tied to our EU funding efforts.

* **Environmental Responsibility (The Grand Plan):**
    * It means having a **zero CO2 impact** from the first year.
    * We actively applied for the **[[FESR Azione 2.2.1 EU Grant]]** grant this morning (**Click Day!**).
    * **The Technology:** This plan includes a high-performance **"Twin Smart-Grid"** project for [[Ville Chiappella]]:
        * **Solar Power:** **20.21 kWp** of solar panels.
        * **Storage:** **42 kWh** of battery storage.
        * **Heating:** Efficient R290 **Heat Pumps**.

* **Resource Independence:**
    * The goal is to produce and **cultivate many things on-site** using our **flat, agricultural riverside plots**.
    * It‚Äôs in our own **long-term interest** to maintain the area.

### **5. If you could share one lesson from your journey with the world, what would it be?**

**Ignore the naysayers and keep building. You can make the impossible happen.**

* **Entrepreneurial Mindset:** This is a phrase often told to entrepreneurs: **"If many tell you it doesn't make sense, you can ignore them and move forward."**
* **Creative Creation:** Entrepreneurship is about **creation**. Many people will tell you that what you are trying to create is **not possible**:
    * *You won't find the houses.*
    * *You won't get the funding.* (We secured two EU grants).
    * *You won't find the people.* (We have a growing community).
* **The Formula:** But if you work **hard**, with the **right skills**, the **right method**, and the **right contacts**, **it can be done!**
* **My Offering:** I now offer **consultancy services** to help others apply for similar grants and achieve their vision.
