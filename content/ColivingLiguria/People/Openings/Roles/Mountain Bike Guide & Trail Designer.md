**[[ColivingLiguria]]** is located a short drive from the **Finale Outdoor** area, a global hotspot for mountain biking. ### The Mission: Trail Builder & MTB Guide

Your role is to develop this new venture from the ground up.
1.  **Trail Design & Building:**
    * Scout, design, and plan a network of Enduro/Downhill trails on the hillside available to us.
    * The top of the hill is accessible by car, making it ideal for shuttle-assisted runs.
    * We will work with you to secure all necessary permits for the land.
2.  **Guiding & Business Development:**
    * Act as the resident MTB guide for [[ColivingLiguria]] guests.
    * Market and attract new guests (riders) to stay at our coliving, who will then be your clients for guided tours.
    * Develop packages (e.g., "Stay & Ride") in collaboration with us.

### Business Model & Earning Potential

This is your business. We provide the land, accommodation, and a base of potential clients; you build the enterprise.
* **Model:** This will be run as your own independent business (Model A from [[Propose a Project]]), where you rent resources from us at a minimal cost and keep the profits from your guiding services.
* **Your Income:** Your revenue will come directly from the guests you guide. You will have the freedom to set your prices and packages.
* **Future Investment:** As this project grows, we have the possibility to use our [[FESR Azione 1.3.3. EU Grant|European funding]] to co-invest in necessary equipment, such as a fleet of rental bikes or tools.

### What We Offer

* **Land & Location:** An entire hillside with forest, accessible by car, ready for development. All this, located right next to the Finale Ligure MTB mecca.
* **Tools & Resources:**
    * All necessary planning and design tools.
    * Availability of an Enduro bike (the owner's, [[ColivingLiguria/People/Simone Testino]]) for you to use.
* **Accommodation:**
    * We offer you a **free or heavily reduced rent** for a room (private or shared) at [[Ville Chiappella]].
    * This will be formalized with a [[Contract for Residents]], ensuring your stay is legally sound.
* **Community & Network:** A vibrant community of international remote workers and volunteers, providing an instant customer base and a great living environment.

### The Ideal Candidate

* Proven experience in trail design and construction (please provide a portfolio).
* Certified MTB guide qualifications.
* Entrepreneurial mindset, motivated to build a business from scratch.
* Good knowledge of the MTB market, especially the Finale Ligure scene.
* Sociable, community-oriented, and ready to live in a rustic, hands-on renovation environment at [[Ville Chiappella]].
* Good level of English is required to communicate with our international guests.
