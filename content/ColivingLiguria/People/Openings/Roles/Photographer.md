> [!Abstract]
> We are looking for an entrepreneurial Photographer to join our community in a dual role. You will act as our **resident photographer**, capturing content for our social media in exchange for free accommodation, and you will have the opportunity to launch your **own independent photography school** as a [[Supplementary Projects|supplementary project]].

This is a unique, flexible role for a skilled photographer. We offer two distinct modes of collaboration that work together:
### 1. The Resident Role: Community Content Creator
As our resident photographer, your primary contribution to the community will be creating visual content. In exchange, you will receive **free or heavily reduced rent** at [[Ville Chiappella]]. This role, formalised with a [[Contract for Residents]], involves:
* **Working with the [[Social Media Manager]]:** You will provide a steady stream of high-quality photos and videos for our social media channels.
* **Documenting the Project:** You will capture the renovation progress (working with plans from our [[Interior Designer]]), community life, and the stunning natural surroundings described in [[Location Ville Chiappella]].

### 2. The Business Role: Photography School Owner
This is your independent venture. We want to help you launch your own business from our hub.
* **Run Your Own Courses:** You can launch and manage your own paid photography courses, leveraging our location and community.
* **Work with our [[Model|Community Face & Photo Model]]:** You will have a dedicated model to collaborate with, allowing you to offer high-value portrait and lifestyle workshops.
* **Business Model:** This is run as your own independent business (Partita IVA / VAT) as per the [[Propose a Project]] framework. You set your prices, bring clients to [[ColivingLiguria]], and you keep the profits. We will help promote your courses via our social media.

### Equipment & Resources
* **Bring Your Own:** It is appreciated if you can bring your own equipment, at least initially.
* **Project-Funded Gear:** We have the possibility to purchase necessary photography and video equipment using our [[FESR Azione 1.3.3. EU Grant]] grant funds.
* **Ownership:** The ownership of any equipment purchased by ColivingLiguria can be negotiated (it can be sole ownership of either party or shared).

### The Ideal Candidate
* A skilled photographer with a strong portfolio (required).
* An entrepreneurial spirit, excited to launch your own teaching business.
* A great collaborator, ready to work closely with our [[Social Media Manager]], [[Model|Community Face & Photo Model]], and [[Interior Designer]].
* A good fit for our community-driven, rustic renovation environment at [[Ville Chiappella]].
* English is required; Italian is a plus.
