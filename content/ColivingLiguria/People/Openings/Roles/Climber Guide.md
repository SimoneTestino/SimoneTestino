> [!Abstract]
> We are looking for a certified Climbing Guide, preferably with route-setting skills, to join our community. This is a unique dual-role opportunity: 1) Design and build on-site bouldering and climbing routes on our house walls and trees. 2) Launch your own independent guiding business, capitalizing on our prime location near the **Finale Ligure** climbing mecca.

**[[ColivingLiguria]]** is located just a short drive from the world-renowned [**Finale Outdoor**](https://www.finaleoutdoor.com/it) area, a global hotspot for climbers, and close to spectacular routes like the [**Via Ferrata degli Artisti**](https://www.ferrate365.it/vie-ferrate/ferrata-artisti-bric-agnellino/).

This creates a massive opportunity for an entrepreneurial guide to establish a base here. We are looking for someone to take charge of this [[Supplementary Projects|project]]. The role is two-fold:

### 1. The On-Site Project: Route Setter
Your first mission is to bring climbing to our home. You will have creative freedom to:
* Design and build bouldering and climbing routes on the external walls of our houses (like [[Casa del Noce]] and [[Casa del Forno]]).
* Create fun, challenging climbing/bouldering circuits on the trees and natural rock formations on our property.
* These routes will serve as a unique amenity and training ground for all our residents.

### 2. The Business: Independent Guide
This is your independent venture. You will be the resident climbing expert for [[ColivingLiguria]].
* **Guiding:** Offer paid guided tours, climbing courses, and experiences to our residents and new guests.
* **Marketing:** Attract climbing tourists to stay at [[ColivingLiguria]] as their base, with you as their dedicated guide. You can create "Stay & Climb" packages in synergy with us.

### Business Model & Compensation
This is your business, run as an independent venture (Model A from [[Propose a Project]]).
* **Your Income:** You keep the profits from your guiding services, courses, and any packages you sell.
* **Accommodation:** In exchange for developing and maintaining the on-site routes and being our resident expert, we offer you **free or heavily reduced rent** for a room at [[Ville Chiappella]].
* **Your Contract:** Your residency will be formalized with a [[Contract for Residents]].

### The Ideal Candidate
* **Certified:** You must be a certified climbing guide (e.g., UIAA, FASI, or equivalent national certification).
* **Skills:** Experience in route setting is a major plus.
* **Entrepreneurial:** You are motivated to build your own business from the ground up.
* **Attitude:** A great collaborator, ready to integrate into our community and live in a rustic, in-progress renovation environment at [[Ville Chiappella]].
* **Language:** English is recommended to communicate with our international community.

### How to Apply
To apply, please send an email to colivingliguria@gmail.com with:
* Your CV and a Motivation Letter.
* Copies of your climbing/guiding certifications are appreciated
* A portfolio of routes you have set (if you have one).
* A brief **Concept Proposal** (as per [[Propose a Project]]) for the guiding business you'd like to run.
