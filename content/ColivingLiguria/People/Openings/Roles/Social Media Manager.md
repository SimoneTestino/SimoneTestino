### Compensation & Business Model

This is not a typical 9-to-5 job; it's an opportunity to build something. The compensation is flexible and depends on your commitment and location.

* **Immediate On-Site Compensation:** For anyone joining us on-site at [[Ville Chiappella]], we offer **free accommodation** (in a private or shared room, to be agreed upon). Your residency will be formalised through a [[Contract for Residents]], ensuring a clear legal framework for your stay.

* **Business & Profit Structure:** We want you to grow with us. The model can be structured as an independent venture, similar to our [[Propose a Project]] framework:
    1.  **Independent Entrepreneur (Model A):** You can operate with your own VAT number (Partita IVA). This allows you to manage and receive income from external sponsorships or collaborations you bring in.
    2.  **Strategic Partner (Long-Term, On-Site Only):** For candidates who commit to a long-term vision and are physically present, we are open to a true partnership. This remuneration can be structured in several ways, similar to our offer for a [[Cofounder for ColivingLiguria Wanted|Cofounder]]:

        * **Profit-Sharing:** A percentage of the project's overall future profits.
        * **Equity:** A path to ownership of ColivingLiguria's assets (the houses). This is reserved for key long-term, on-site partners.
