> [!Abstract]
> We are looking for a passionate and creative Community Cook to take charge of our kitchen at [[Ville Chiappella]]. This role is central to our community life, with flexible models: you can cook for residents in exchange for your stay, or launch your own independent business offering paid courses and special meals.

A great kitchen is the heart of a great community. We are looking for a cook to manage our communal meals, create a wonderful dining experience, and work in close synergy with our [[Farmer]] to use fresh, on-site produce.

### The Role: Community or Entrepreneur?
We offer two main pathways for this role, which can be combined:

1.  **Core Resident (The Community Cook)**
    * **The Mission:** You cook for the community. Your contribution is managing the communal meals (e.g., one large shared dinner per day) for all residents.
    * **Compensation:** You live at [[Ville Chiappella]] with **free or heavily reduced rent**, formalized with a [[Contract for Residents]].

2.  **Independent Business (The Chef-preneur)**
    * **The Mission:** You can launch your own food-based ventures as [[Supplementary Projects]]. This could include:
        * Offering paid **cooking classes** to residents.
        * Selling special, high-end meals, or offering a private catering service.
    * **Compensation:** This requires a formal business structure (like a Partita IVA in Italy) and a specific agreement with me ([[ColivingLiguria/People/Simone Testino]]) as outlined in [[Propose a Project]]. You earn directly from these activities.

### The Ideal Candidate
* **Skills:** You are a great cook with a passion for communal dining.
* **Vegan-Friendly:** **Experience with vegan recipes is highly preferred.** We anticipate that up to one-third of our residents may be non-omnivores.
* **Adaptable:** You will be provided with all data on residents' allergies, intolerances, and preferences to help you create meals for everyone.
* **Collaborative:** You are excited to work directly with the [[Farmer]] to plan menus based on seasonal produce.
* **Attitude:** You are a good fit for our community-driven, rustic renovation environment at [[Ville Chiappella]].
* **Language:** English is preferred for communication in our international community.

### How to Apply
To apply, please send an email to **colivingliguria@gmail.com** with:
* Your CV and a Motivation Letter.
* **Portfolio/Examples:** Please include photos of dishes you've made, sample menus, or any other proof of your culinary skills.
* A brief note on which model (Core Resident or Independent Business) interests you most.
