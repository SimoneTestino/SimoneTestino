> [!Abstract]
> We are looking for an entrepreneurial and creative Farmer to take charge of our agricultural production. This role has immense freedom, from managing vegetable gardens to planting orchards and raising livestock. We offer flexible models: work as a resident for free rent, build your own business by selling produce, and for long-term partners, a path to co-ownership of the land itself.

At [[ColivingLiguria]], our extensive outdoor spaces are managed by three distinct, synergistic roles:
1.  **[[Gardener]]**: Focuses on the aesthetic and leisure gardens around the houses.
2.  **[[Woodland Steward]]**: Manages our wild forests for creative projects and sustainable resources.
3.  **Farmer (This Role)**: Focuses on **production**‚Äîgrowing food, managing livestock, and creating agricultural value from our lands.

### The Vision: Your Farm, Your Creativity

This role requires a high degree of creativity and autonomy. You will largely decide what to do and how to do it. You will set your own goals, in dialogue with the project's needs.

Your "fields" are varied and full of potential:
* **Vegetable Gardens:** Start with the existing *orticello* (small garden) at **[[Casa del Forno]]** and expand into new plots, like the large garden at **[[Casa del Noce]]**.
* **Agricultural Land:** The flat, streamside plots of **[[Terreni oltre Torrente]]** are perfect for larger-scale cultivation of vegetables or other crops.
* **Forests & Orchards:** Our woodlands ([[Terreni Lungotorrente]], etc.) are ideal for planting fruit and nut trees or for sustainable firewood harvesting.
* **Livestock:** We are **especially interested in proposals that involve raising livestock**. We have the space and are excited to support a partner willing to manage this long-term.

### Synergies & Support
* **The [[Cook]]:** You will work in close synergy with our future **[[Cook]]** to supply the Coliving kitchen with fresh, on-site produce.
* **Regulations:** For any project (especially livestock), you must adhere to local regulations. The founder, **[[ColivingLiguria/People/Simone Testino]]**, will personally assist with all bureaucratic matters and translate all necessary rules into English to be consulted on this site, so we can develop the project together.

### Compensation & Business Models

We offer multiple, flexible pathways for this role:

1.  **Core Resident Model:** You can live at [[Ville Chiappella]] with **free or heavily reduced rent**, managing the farm as your primary contribution to the community under a [[Contract for Residents]].
2.  **Independent Business (Model A):** Use your own VAT number (Partita IVA). You can earn income by:
    * Selling produce to residents or local markets.
    * Creating internal agreements and synergies (e.g., selling directly to the [[Cook]]'s budget).
3.  **Long-Term Equity (In Addition):** For individuals who commit to a long-term plan (especially for livestock or perennial crops), we offer a path to **co-ownership of the agricultural lands** you manage.

**Ownership of Products:** The ownership of the crops and livestock is also flexible. Depending on the contract we stipulate together, they can be 100% yours (if you run an independent business) or shared between you and ColivingLiguria.

### The Ideal Candidate
* **Highly creative, autonomous, and self-motivated.**
* Experienced in agriculture, permaculture, or livestock management.
* **We strongly prefer candidates with long-term plans**, especially those involving livestock.
* A collaborative spirit, excited to work with the [[Cook]] and other land managers.
* Prepared for the rustic, in-progress renovation environment at [[Ville Chiappella]].
* English is required; Italian is a plus.

### How to Apply
To apply, please send an email to **colivingliguria@gmail.com** with:
* Your CV and a motivation letter.
* **A Concept Proposal:** This is essential. Following the [[Propose a Project]] guidelines, send us a brief document with your *initial ideas* for the farm.
