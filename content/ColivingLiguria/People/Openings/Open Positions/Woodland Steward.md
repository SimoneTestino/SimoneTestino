> [!Abstract]
> We are looking for a creative and inventive **Woodland Steward** to manage our forested lands. This role is a blank canvas for someone to develop unique projects, from creating nature oases to new business ventures. This is a resident-based position offering free or reduced rent, with a long-term path to potential equity in the woodlands themselves.

This is not a typical land management role. At [[ColivingLiguria]], our outdoor spaces are managed by three distinct roles:
1.  **[[Farmer]]**: Focuses on agriculture and food production.
2.  **[[Gardener]]**: Focuses on the aesthetic and leisure gardens around the houses.
3.  **Woodland Steward (This Role)**: Focuses on the creative and sustainable development of our wilder, forested lands.

### The Vision: A Blank Slate for Our Woods

We are looking for someone to take ownership of our forests, primarily the [[Terreni Lungotorrente]] ("Lands Along the Stream"), [[Terreni alla Fonte]] ("Lands at the Source"), and [[Terreni oltre Torrente]] ("Lands Beyond the Stream").

As their names suggest, these properties are rich with water sources ("rigoli d'acqua" or rivulets). This role has the most creative freedom and "carta bianca" of any position at ColivingLiguria. We want you to be inventive, set your own goals, and unlock the potential of these lands.

### Key Project Areas (Examples of Your Mission)

* **Creative Spaces:** In synergy with the [[Farmer]], design and build unique resources in the woods:
    * Small, hidden offices for remote workers.
    * Creative areas for artists (meditation platforms, land art).
* **Resource Management:**
    * Sustainably harvest resources like chestnuts.
    * Explore potential for raising livestock.
* **Collaboration:** Work closely with the **[[Mountain Bike Guide & Trail Designer]]** to ensure trails are integrated beautifully and sustainably into the forest.
* **Expansion:** Your creativity is our engine for growth. If we find successful, sustainable applications for our woods, we will look to acquire more and expand this part of the project.

### Compensation & Business Model
This opportunity is offered primarily as a **resident-based position**.
* **Accommodation:** We offer **heavily reduced or null rent** for a place at [[Ville Chiappella]], formalized with a [[Contract for Residents]].
* **Long-Term Partnership:** This role is crucial for our long-term vision. For the right person who commits to the project, we offer a unique compensation path: the opportunity to **gain equity and co-ownership of the woodlands themselves**.
* **Freedom:** You will be a full member of the community, free to participate in (or propose) any other **[[Supplementary Projects]]**.

### Process & Collaboration
While you have creative freedom, all projects must be developed transparently and in synergy with the founder, **[[ColivingLiguria/People/Simone Testino]]**. You will be expected to follow the formal process outlined on our **[[Propose a Project]]** page to bring your ideas to life.

### The Ideal Candidate
* **Highly creative, inventive, and self-motivated.** You are comfortable setting your own goals.
* You have a passion for nature, ecology, and sustainable land use.
* Skills in permaculture, forestry, land art, or landscape design are a huge plus, but we value creativity and vision above all.
* A collaborative spirit, ready to work with the Farmer and MTB Guide.
* Prepared for a rustic, in-progress renovation environment at [[Ville Chiappella]].
* English is required; Italian is a plus.
