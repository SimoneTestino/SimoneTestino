### ‚ú® The First Volunteer Opportunities
We are looking for **two volunteers** to join us immediately for this foundational phase. This is a unique chance to be involved from the very beginning as we set up the house and the project. The expected work time is 6 hours per day. The two roles are:

1. **Community & Operations Support (Hands-On):**
This person will be the heart of our daily life. The main tasks will be hands-on and focus on creating a comfortable and welcoming home. This includes the periodic cleaning of the bathroom, floors, and kitchen. A major and rewarding task will be the gardening; although the garden is not huge (under 400 m¬≤), it is not in good condition and needs a proper renovation plan to make it look nice. This role also involves cooking. At the beginning, we will have shared meals, so this person will prepare lunch and dinner to ensure we can all eat together and that food is always ready for those who are working. They will also be responsible for keeping the kitchen clean at all times.

2. **Digital Storyteller & Project Support (Hybrid):**
	This is a hybrid role. A minimum of three hours per day will be dedicated to building our social media presence, with the remaining hours devoted to hands-on tasks like cleaning and gardening, which are essential at the start. The social media duties are crucial and include:
	* **Instagram Management:** Posting stories, reels, and updates on a daily basis to keep our community informed about our progress, interesting things in the area, and developments in the project.
	* **Facebook Recruiting:** Actively managing our Facebook presence, responding to comments and posts, and handling inquiries from potential future volunteers and clients.

### üìù Project Plan & Next Steps
Our next steps are focused on detailed planning to transform this house into a fully operational coliving space. Our interior designer, **Parisa**, will lead the creation of a comprehensive design plan to renovate the house and make it look its best. This will involve taking detailed measurements of the entire building to create a complete 3D model in Fusion 360. This digital twin will allow us to control every aspect of the renovation and plan any changes with precision.

In parallel, I will be drafting the long-term contract. The plan is to establish a rent-to-buy agreement where the profits from renting rooms to remote workers will go to the owner, plus a premium, until the property becomes ours. We foresee this process taking no longer than a year from now.

It is important to note that two of the five apartments are currently rented out. The owner is confident the tenants will leave very soon, though we suspect it may take longer. Since the apartments are totally separate from where we will be staying, this does not affect our immediate plans. In fact, we see this as a positive, as it will likely be a good point of negotiation to lower the final price of the property.
