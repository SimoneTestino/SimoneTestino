### Important: Payment Description (Causale)
It is **mandatory** to include a clear description (causale) with every bank transfer, regardless of any prior verbal or written agreements.
* **If you have a contract:** You *must* include the unique Identifier Number of your [[Contract for Residents]].
* **If you do not have a contract yet:** You *must* write a clear explanation of what the payment is for and what you expect in return (e.g., "Security Deposit for [Your Name] - Nov 2025").
* **Rule of Thumb:** Never assume we know what a payment is for. Always write it down clearly in the transaction notes.

### Timing and Deadlines
Please be aware that many bank transfers are not instant. It may take several business days for funds to clear, especially for international transfers.
* **Deadline Policy:** For any payment with a deadline (e.g., securing a booking), we only consider the **time of actual receipt** into our bank account, *not* the time you initiated the transfer. Please plan accordingly to ensure funds arrive on time.

### Official Receipts
Transparency is paramount. Every valid payment received by Coliving Liguria will be followed by an official digital receipt.
* **The Process:** You will receive an email from our Certified Email address (PEC): `simone.testino@legalmail.it`.
* **The Content:** This email will contain a standard PDF receipt and a legally binding **.p7m file** (digitally signed), detailing exactly what we received, how it has been recorded in our system, and what that payment guarantees you in return.
* **Timing:** By Italian law, we must send this within 10 days, but we typically aim to send it **within 24 hours** of receiving the funds.
* **Did not receive it?** If you do not receive this official PEC email within 24 hours of when you believe the funds should have arrived, please [[Contact]] me, [[ColivingLiguria/People/Simone Testino|Simone Testino]], immediately to verify the transaction.
