### Phase 1: Project Start & First Week (From Dec 3rd)

* **The Start (Dec 3rd, 16:00):** The deed will be signed, and the keys transferred. The founder, [[ColivingLiguria/People/Simone Testino]], will be legally residing in the houses from that evening.
* **The First Week (Dec 3rd - 10th):** This will be an intensive "setup" phase. The immediate priority will be on deep cleaning and activating essential utilities (water, electricity, heating, hot water). This initial setup is detailed in our **[[Plan for the First Days]]**.
* **New Arrivals:** While the project officially opens on Dec 3rd, new residents are advised to arrive **from the following week (e.g., Dec 10th)** for a more comfortable experience, once utilities are stable.

### Phase 2: Bookings & Team Onboarding

* **Bookings Now Open:** We are **actively accepting bookings** for arrivals starting **December 3rd, 2025, through mid-2026**.
* **High Demand:** We are receiving many requests. Spaces are limited and will be assigned based on our [[Admission Priorities]]. We advise booking immediately.
* **How to Join:** All arrivals must follow the **[[Onboarding]]** process and sign the **[[Contract for Residents]]**. We are already signing these contracts, which are conditional upon the successful purchase on Dec 3rd.
* **Roles:** We are booking for [[Volunteers Ville Chiappella]], [[Digital Nomads Ville Chiappella]], and partners for our [[Openings]].

### Other Project Updates

* **Nov 14:** New photos of the [[Ville Chiappella]] houses will be taken. The **[[Marketing Team]]** will begin processing them over the weekend for social media.
* **Nov 14:** [[Emanuele Garré]] will visit the properties in **Mignanego** (the subject of our other analysis) to take photos and assess their potential. This is a separate, potential side-project, as the lands are not nearby, but could be acquired for free.

* **Side Projects:** Our [[Supplementary Projects]] with project partners are also moving forward.
