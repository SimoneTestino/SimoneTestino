> This note outlines a potential collaboration regarding properties in Mignanego, owned by relatives of a friend of [[ColivingLiguria/People/Simone Testino]]. These assets may be made available to [[ColivingLiguria]] for development.

### Collaboration Framework

* **Short-Term:** The properties can be made available for ColivingLiguria projects under an informal, oral **"Comodato D'uso Gratuito"** (free loan for use).
