- **Category:** A/4 (Residential)

### Garden and Exteriors (Mappale 522)
The extensive outdoor space is a primary asset of this property, providing both leisure space and crucial building volume.

- **Land Registry:** Foglio 29, Particella 522 (**Total Area: 1,270 m²**)
- **Zoning (CDU):**
    * **Zone Bp 4 (Residential):** Part of the land allows for residential construction (Index $0.4 \, \text{mc/mq}$).
    * **Zone E (Agricultural):** Part of the land allows for agricultural structures (Index $0.03 \, \text{mc/mq}$).
- **Key Features:** A crucial **right of way for vehicle access** is present, allowing vehicles and campers to park directly on the property. The garden contains a large, healthy **walnut tree** (noce) and receives fruit from an overhanging **fig tree** (fico).
