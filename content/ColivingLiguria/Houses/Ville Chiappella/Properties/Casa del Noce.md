This document provides a detailed breakdown of the "Casa del Noce" property, located at STRADA CHIAPPELLA, Comune di CAIRO MONTENOTTE (SV). The property consists of a main residential building, a large rustic outbuilding, and a significant garden.

### Main House (Particella 519, Sub. 6 & Particella 520)

- **Land Registry:** Foglio 29, Particella 519 (Sub. 6) & 520
- **Category:** A/4 (Residential)
- **Surface Area:** 59 m²
- **Floor Plan:** [[PLANIMETRIA FG 29 MAPP 519 SUB 6.pdf]] & [[PLANIMETRIA FG 29 MAPP 519 SUB 6 1.pdf]]
- **Structural Condition:** The main structure is solid and in good condition, built with a characteristic mix of stone and brick. The roof is functional, with main wooden beams in excellent shape. The street-side facade has minor, non-structural cracks and features an aesthetically pleasing stone portion. The garden-side facade is almost entirely stone with high visual potential.

### Rustic Outbuilding (Particella 521)
A large, independent rustic building located within the garden, offering significant potential for future development (e.g., "Progetto Luna").

- **Land Registry:** Foglio 29, Particella 521
- **Category:** C/2 (Warehouse/Storage)
- **Surface Area:** 60 m²
- **Floor Plan:** [[PLANIMETRIA FG 29 MAPP 521.pdf]]
- **Structural Condition:** The building is structurally sound and straight, with a solid base of stone and brick. The roof structure is holding up well, though some metal sheet additions need removal. The interior is in a raw state, without flooring, but the upper floor slab is rigid and in good condition.

### Garden and Exteriors (Mappale 522)
The extensive outdoor space is a primary asset of this property.

- **Key Features:** A crucial **right of way for vehicle access** is present, allowing vehicles and campers to park directly on the property. The garden (~1200 m²) contains a large, healthy **walnut tree** (noce) and receives fruit from an overhanging **fig tree** (fico). A small dry-stone wall in front of the house requires rebuilding for safety. An external masonry staircase, supported by a healthy wooden structure, connects the ground floor to the first floor.

### Rustico (Particella 521)
Un grande edificio rustico indipendente situato all'interno del giardino, che offre un notevole potenziale per sviluppi futuri (es. "Progetto Luna").

- **Dati Catastali:** Foglio 29, Particella 521
- **Categoria:** C/2 (Magazzino/Deposito)
- **Superficie:** 60 m²
- **Planimetria:** [[PLANIMETRIA FG 29 MAPP 521.pdf]]

### Giardino ed Esterni (Mappale 522)
L'ampio spazio esterno è il principale valore aggiunto di questa proprietà.

- **Caratteristiche Chiave:** È presente una fondamentale **servitù di diritto di accesso carrabile**, che permette a veicoli e camper di parcheggiare direttamente sulla proprietà. Il giardino (circa 1200 m²) ospita un grande **noce** in ottima salute e riceve i frutti da un **fico** i cui rami sporgono sulla proprietà. Un piccolo muretto a secco di fronte alla casa necessita di essere ricostruito per sicurezza. Una scala esterna in muratura, sorretta da una sana struttura in legno, collega il piano terra al primo piano.
