We are looking for talented artists to perform for our international community and guests. This is more than just a gig; it's a chance to connect, collaborate, and perform in a truly special outdoor setting.

### What is "Music Saturday"?

This is our new flagship event, planned for the **first Friday and Saturday of every month**. We reserve these two days on our [[Periodic Events]] calendar for live music. We can host **several bands or artists** over the weekend.

* **When:** The first Friday & Saturday of every month (slots are available for booking).
* **Where:** On our custom-built outdoor stage at [[Ville Chiappella]]. We have two main venue options:
    1.  The **[[Casa del Noce]] Garden** (1,270 m¬≤).
    2.  The **[[Terreni oltre Torrente]]** (1,240 m¬≤ of flat riverside land).
* **The Vibe:** An intimate, open-air concert for our residents and external guests.
* **Weather:** All concerts are **weather-dependent**. In case of bad weather, we must reschedule.

### What We Offer You

We want you to focus on your music, so we provide the core infrastructure for free:

* **A Unique Venue:** Free access to one of our large outdoor performance spaces, including a stage.
* **Sound System:** We provide the essential PA system: **speakers, stands, and a basic mixer**.
* **Free Accommodation:** The performing artist (or band, up to 4 members) gets **free accommodation** in our coliving house for the night of the event.
* **Free Transport (Local):** We will organize a **free shuttle service for you and your instruments** from the [[How to get here|Cairo Montenotte (Station)]] to our venue.
* **A Built-in Audience:** You'll perform for our diverse community. Our [[Marketing Team]] will also promote the event on all our [[Social Media]] channels.
* **Audience Stays:** We have accommodation options for your fans who want to stay overnight (they must register via [[Event Registration]]).

### What You Bring üé∏

* **Your Talent:** Vocals, loops, or your full band.
* **Your Instruments:** You must bring your own guitars, keyboards, drum kits, controllers, etc..
* **Your Vibe:** A passion for sharing your music and connecting with new people.

### How to Apply (The Process)

This is a collaborative event, managed by our [[Event Organisation Team]], so we need to formalise it.

1.  **Read the Rules:** This is a formal event. Please first read our main **[[Propose an Event]]** guidelines to understand the approval process.
2.  **Send Your Proposal:** Email us at **[colivingliguria@gmail.com](mailto:colivingliguria@gmail.com)** with the subject "Music Saturday Proposal". Include:
    * A link to your music (SoundCloud, Spotify, YouTube, etc.).
    * Your technical requirements (e.g., "we are a 3-piece band and need 3 mic inputs").
    * The dates you are available (first Friday/Saturday of the month).
3.  **Define the Model & Contract:** We will discuss which model fits best:
    * **Marketing-Driven (Free):** Our preferred model. The concert is 100% free for everyone. You get the full package (venue, transport, accommodation, promotion) in exchange for your performance.
    * **Financial-Driven (Ticketed):** If you wish to charge an entry fee. This is fine, but **all coliving residents must attend for free**. The contract will define a fair percentage split of the ticket revenue.
    * We will then send you the official **[[Contract for Music Event Proposals]]**. This simple document protects both of us and outlines all the details we've agreed upon.
