> [!Abstract]
> The Saturday Pizza Night is our main weekly community event. Led by our professional [[Cook]], it's a chance to learn how to make real Italian pizza, share a meal in the garden of [[Casa del Noce]], and connect with all the residents.

### Our Weekly Community Tradition
Every Saturday evening, we host our most important social event: the Pizza Night. It's a fun, hands-on tradition where we all come together to make and eat authentic Italian pizza.

### The Food & The Process
This isn't just any pizza. We are led by our professional [[Cook]], who prepares the dough and ingredients. We use the large, traditional wood-fired oven located in front of [[Casa del Forno]] to get the perfect result.

### How to Join: The Weekly Plan
Participation is easy, but requires a little planning:
* **Every Tuesday:** The [[Cook]] will send a poll to the **[[Whatsapp Community#III. üè° In Loco|In Loco üè°]]** group chat.
* **The Poll:** In the poll, you must confirm if you are joining and select your pizza. There are always multiple options, including **guaranteed vegetarian and vegan choices**.
* **The Deadline:** You must confirm your choice by **Friday at 12:00 (12:00 PM)**.
* **Why the deadline?** After this time, the [[Cook]] will buy the fresh ingredients and begin preparations based on the exact number of orders. No changes can be made after this point.

### Timeline & Helping Out
The meal is a group effort, and all help is encouraged!
* **15:00 - 16:00 (3:00 PM - 4:00 PM):** The [[Cook]] begins the main preparations.
* **From 16:00 (4:00 PM) onwards:** Residents are welcome (and encouraged!) to come and help. You can learn how to stretch the dough, prepare toppings, or help set the tables. Please text the [[Cook]] if you plan to come early.
* **19:00 (7:00 PM):** The meal begins! We eat together at the long tables in the main garden of [[Casa del Noce]].
* **22:30 (10:30 PM):** Official event end. By this time, everyone is expected to have helped clean up. All communal dishes and areas must be clean and tidy. A designated person will be responsible for ensuring this happens. Guests can stay longer, but they are then responsible for their own items and cleaning up after themselves.

This event is the heart of our community life, and we expect all residents to take part, not just in eating, but in the fun of preparing and cleaning up together.
