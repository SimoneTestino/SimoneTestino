### A Home for You and Your Horse
At [[ColivingLiguria]], we don't just accept pets; we build for them. We are creating a unique environment where you can live, work remotely, and keep your horse right on your doorstep.

### The Facilities: Comfort & Technology

We are constructing a dedicated **[[Horse Shelter]]** to ensure maximum comfort in all seasons.
* **Winter:** The structure will be fully enclosed and insulated to protect against the cold.
* **Summer:** A forced ventilation system will keep the shaded areas cool and airy.
* **Technology:** The stable will be energy-autonomous (solar panels) and connected to the internet, featuring internal cameras so you can monitor your horse from your room.

### Capacity & Demand
* **Current Limit:** To ensure the highest quality of life and compliance with regulations, our current capacity is strictly limited to **3 Horses**.
* **High Demand:** We already have bookings for the upcoming season. If you are interested, **you must let us know well in advance**. Last-minute requests are unlikely to be accommodated.

Living here means more than just stabling. We organize:
* **Walks & Trekking:** Explore the stunning Ligurian woods with your horse.
* **Community Projects:** We are developing **[[Supplementary Projects]]** centered around equine activities.
* **Updates:** We will soon publish more details on specific services and horse-handling assistance directly on this page.

### How to Apply
Hosting a horse is a significant responsibility. To ensure everything is legal and safe:
1.  Read the **[[Contract for Horses]]** page to understand the procedure.
2.  Review the **[[Normative Detenzione Equini]]** to ensure your documents are in order.
3.  **Contact Us:** Reach out directly to the founder, **[[ColivingLiguria/People/Simone Testino]]**, to discuss your needs and check availability.
