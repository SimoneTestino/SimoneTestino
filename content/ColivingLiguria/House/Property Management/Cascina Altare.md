## Project and Works

### Project Strategy & Timeline
Our acquisition strategy is a rent-to-buy programme, as it is unlikely that the multiple co-owners will fund the renovation. We will finance the works ourselves in exchange for future ownership.

* **By 16th August:** Sign the temporary 'loan-for-use' (`comodato`) agreement.
* **By 31st August:** During the initial `comodato` period, we will:
    1.  Have the property professionally valued.
    2.  Draft the definitive long-term acquisition contract (rent-to-buy).
    3.  Finalise plans with long-term volunteers who intend to stay at the property.
* **Post 31st August:** Based on the valuation and project plan, we will make a formal rent-to-buy offer. The payment structure will likely be a fixed rate or profit-based payments (e.g., every 6 months) until an agreed-upon threshold is met.
