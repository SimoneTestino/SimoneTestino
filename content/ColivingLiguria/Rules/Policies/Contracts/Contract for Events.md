> This page introduces the Event Organisation Team at [[ColivingLiguria]]. This team is the central point of contact for all event-related activities, from official proposals via the [[Propose an Event]] page to managing the [[Event Registration]] process and ensuring events run smoothly.

Events are a vital part of our community. The Event Organisation Team's mission is to ensure that every event is safe, well-managed, financially sound, and aligned with the project's long-term goals. We manage the entire lifecycle of an event, from the initial idea to the final cleanup.

### Meet the Team
