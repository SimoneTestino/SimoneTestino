### This Contract is a Collaboration

We are in the very early stages of our project, and our legal frameworks are being built step-by-step. We do not have a standard, one-size-fits-all PDF contract for music events *yet*.

Our goal is to create the first contract **together with the first artist or band** that wants to play here. This allows us to build a fair, simple, and effective agreement that works for both parties.

### The Two Event Models

We will base our contract on one of two simple models:

1.  **The Marketing-Driven Model (Free Concert)**
    * This is for artists who want to play for community and marketing benefits.
    * **You get:** A free venue, sound system, promotion on our [[Social Media]], and free accommodation.
    * **We get:** A great live music event for our community.
    * **The Contract:** Will be very simple, formalising that the event is free for all attendees and no fees will be exchanged.

2.  **The Financial-Driven Model (Ticketed Concert)**
    * This applies if you wish to charge an entry fee for external guests or have other financial expectations.
    * **The Contract:** This will be a more detailed profit-sharing agreement. It will clearly state:
        * All residents of Coliving Liguria attend for free.
        * A clear percentage split of ticket revenue between you and the Coliving.
        * Who manages the ticketing (via our [[Event Registration]] system).

### Related Legal Documents

This contract will work alongside our other standard agreements. You should be familiar with them:

* **[[Propose an Event]]**: Your proposal must first be sent and approved according to this process.
* **[[Contract for Events]]**: This is the separate contract that *all attendees* (your fans, our guests) must sign to be on the property during the event.

Once we have drafted the first prototype of the *Contract for Concerts* PDF, we will upload it here. Until then, please get in touch directly!
