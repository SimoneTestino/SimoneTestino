### This Contract is a Collaboration

This is the legal agreement between **you (the Organizer)** and **Coliving Liguria**. It is different from the [[Contract for Events]], which is the separate agreement signed by all *attendees*.

We are in the very early stages of our project. We do not have a fixed PDF template for every type of event yet. Our goal is to create the first contract (e.g., for the first yoga retreat, or the first tech workshop) **together with you, the first organiser**. This allows us to build a fair, simple, and effective agreement.

### The Two Event Models

We will base our contract on one of two simple models:

1.  **The Marketing-Driven Model (Free Event)**
    * This is for organizers who want to host an event for community and marketing benefits (e.g., a free skills-sharing workshop).
    * **You get:** A free venue, promotion on our [[Social Media]], and potentially free accommodation.
    * **We get:** A great event for our community.
    * **The Contract:** Will be very simple, formalising that the event is free for all attendees and defining the resources we provide.

2.  **The Financial-Driven Model (Ticketed/Paid Event)**
    * This applies if you wish to charge an entry fee (e.g., for a paid multi-day retreat or workshop).
    * **The Contract:** This will be a more detailed profit-sharing agreement. It will clearly state:
        * All residents of Coliving Liguria attend for free.
        * A clear percentage split of ticket revenue between you and Coliving Liguria.
        * Who manages the ticketing (via our [[Event Registration]] system).

### Related Legal Documents

This contract is one part of our legal framework. You should also be aware of:

* **[[Propose an Event]]**: The main policy page you must follow to get your idea approved *before* we can write this contract.
* **[[Contract for Events]]**: The separate contract that *all your attendees* (both residents and external guests) must sign to be on the property.
* **[[Contracts]]**: Our main index page explaining our legal philosophy.

Once we have drafted the first prototype of a general *Contract for Event Proposals* PDF, we will upload it here. Until then, please get in touch directly!
